## Registration with Email
You can register to `SyncFlow` with your email address. To do so, follow these steps:

1. Visit the [SyncFlow Registration Page](https://syncflow.live/signup).

<div className="text-center p-2">
    <img src="/docs/signup.png" alt="Sign Up" className="mx-auto"/>
</div>

2. Fill out the above form, and Click the `Register` button.

If the signup is successful, you will be shown the following screen:
<div className="text-center p-2">
    <img src="/docs/signup-success.png" alt="Sign Up Success" className="mx-auto" />
</div>

You can now log in to the SyncFlow dashboard using the email and password you provided during registration, using the [Login Page](https://syncflow.live/login).

## Registration with GitHub
You can also register to `SyncFlow` with your GitHub account. To do so, follow these steps:

1. Visit the [SyncFlow Registration Page](https://syncflow.live/signup).

2. Click on the `GitHub` icon to sign up with your GitHub account. You will be redirected to GitHub to authorize the application.
<div className="text-center p-2">
    <img src="/docs/github-oauth.png" alt="GitHub OAuth" className="mx-auto" />
</div>

Upon success, you will be redirected back to the SyncFlow dashboard.
